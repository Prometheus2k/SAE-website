About Us

SAE GEC Thrissur

SAE stands for the Society Of Automotive Engineers and is an internationally active professional association for Engineering professionals and students.

SAE GEC Thrissur, a collegiate member of SAEINDIA began in 2016 when an enthusiastic group of our seniors wanted to pursue engineering that was taught beyond their curriculum and texts. There were difficulties at first, but hard work and determination got us through. In 2019, Team-BHP came in 3rd for the tractor design competition conducted by SAEISS and put us on the map. Since then, there has been no looking back and the club has been the cornerstone in our institution for the development of industry-relevant skills and etiquette. As a result, the club grew tremendously and has been involved and awarded in several major projects such as M-Baja, E-Baja, TDC, ADC, BDC, ETWDC, Efficycle, and so on. We intend to develop and nurture a much-needed engineering culture among students. And sure enough, we plan to learn new information that others can bring to the table as well.

The Society Of Automotive Engineers India is India's leading resource for mobility technology. As an individual member-driven society of mobility practitioners, the ownership of SAEINDIA rests with its members who are Individuals from the mobility community, which includes Engineers, Executives from Industry, Government Officials, Academicians and Students. SAEINDIA is a strategic alliance partner of SAE International registered in India as an Indian nonprofit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of the mobility industry in India. SAEINDIA is divided into four sections - Bengaluru section, Northern & Eastern action, Southern section and the Western section.

GEC Thrissur

Government Engineering College, Thrissur was established in 1957 and is one of the premier colleges in Kerala and currently has an MHRD National Institutional ranking of 164. Since its inception, the college has shown enormous growth in all fronts and currently, it is one of the most sought after colleges in engineering education with seven full-fledged UG programmes, six part-time UG programmes, ten PG programmes and PhD research programmes in Engineering. Besides the engineering programmes, the college also offers UG and PG programmes in Architecture and PG programme in Computer Applications (MCA). MBAJA

MBAJA is an event for the undergraduate engineering students, organized globally by the Society of Automotive Engineers, USA. The event originated in the name of Mini - BAJA, in the year 1976 at the University of Carolina. Since then, the event has spanned across six countries – USA, Mexico, South Africa, Korea, Brazil and India. MBAJA SAE tasks the students to design, fabricate and validate a single-seater four-wheeled off-road vehicle to take part in a series of events that test the vehicle for the sound engineering practices, the agility of the vehicle in terms of gradability, speed, acceleration, endurance, durability and manoeuverability characteristics.

EBAJA

E-Baja is a student off-road vehicle design competition, organised by SAEINDIA. Teams of students need to design and build electric-powered ATV. E-BAJA is a step ahead in gradual liberalization of the automobile sector in India and is a strong approach towards shaping the future direction of the auto industry to promote and achieve sustainable growth. All entries in this category run on rechargeable lithium-ion batteries.

TDC

Tractor Design Competition tasks the students to think, analyse, design, develop, build, test and present a tractor in a series of events. Provides students practical technical experience in the design of drive train systems, tractor performance and manufacturing processes

ADC

Aero Design Challenge is a competition for designing and fabricating a fixed-wing electric motor propelled radio-controlled aircraft. The purpose is to promote and develop Indian expertise and experience in unmanned systems technologies. The competition is being conducted by SAE India Southern Section since 2017.

The event is conducted in 2 categories:

  • 1. Regular Class
  • 2. Micro Class

EWTDC

Electric Two-Wheeler Design Competition prepares the students to be effective professional engineers and provides a platform to students for enhancing skill set, competency and technical know-how in the electrification of two-wheelers. Students gain practical technical experience in the design of electric drive train & control systems, two-wheeler performance, manufacturing processes of assemblies and parts, simulation of drive conditions and integration of all-electric drive systems.

SUPRA

SUPRA is being organised annually by SAEINDIA with the support of Maruti Suzuki. This event provides a platform for students to apply their engineering skills to design and construct a Formula category vehicle as per defined performance and safety specifications

CONVENTION

The Student Convention is conducted by SAEISS. The objective is to celebrate success of year long activities that happened at collegiate club, showcase students' abilities, share the experiences and networks, and motivate students to take up career in automotive sciences. As a part of this, various competitions and events are conducted in 3 tiers namely Tier I (College level), Tier 2 (Divisional level) and Tier 3 (Main convention).

BDC

The bicycle design competition has the objective of giving a first-hand opportunity to the student members to conceive, design, implement and operate a bicycle from scratch. The bicycle design competition allows undergraduate engineering students to cope with real-life engineering challenges.

Membership

By having a student membership in SAEINDIA, students can participate in various competitions and also attend conferences and webinars conducted by SAEINDIA. It is a great opportunity to connect with industry leaders, enhance your skills and leadership experience. In addition to this, the members can access Automotive and Engineering magazines, and SAEINDIA's Newsletter featuring society events, industry news, job openings, and more.

Students belonging to any year can take annual membership. But in order to participate in events like BAJA, SUPRA, annual membership will be invalid. Moreover, the annual membership expires by March every year.
